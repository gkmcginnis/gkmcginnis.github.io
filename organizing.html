First Order Retrievability

Adam Savage talked about the importance of this while working in the shop at ILM (Industrial Light and Margic). Creating his own toolbox, he customized everything to fit his needs and fit his tools exactly. Every tool was accessible without having to move another tool. That is what first order retrievability is.
